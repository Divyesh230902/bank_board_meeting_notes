 **Minutes of 586th Central Board Meeting: December 18, 2020**

**Place:** Mumbai through Video Conference

**Items Discussed:**

1. Signing of Minutes of the meeting of the Central Board held on October 23, 2020 - *Secretary's Department*
2. Grant of leave of absence to Directors - *Secretary's Department*
3. Weekly Statement of Affairs (DGBA) of Reserve Bank of India for the week ended December 11, 2020 - *Department of Government and Bank Accounts (DGBA)*
4. Submission of Proceedings of the meetings of the Committees of the Central Board - *Secretary's Department*
5. Weekly Financial and Commercial Report (WFCR) - *Department of Economic and Policy Research (DEPR)*
6. RBI General Regulations 1949-Amendment - *Human Resources Management Department (HRMD)*
7. Meetings of the Central Board of the Reserve Bank of India: Calendar of meetings:2021-22 - *Secretary's Department*
8. Report on the activities of the Financial Inclusion and Development Department for the period October 1, 2019 to September 30, 2020 - *Financial Inclusion and Development Department (FIDD)*
9. Report on the activities of the Foreign Exchange Department for the period October 1, 2019 to September 30, 2020 - *Foreign Exchange Department (FED)*
10. Minutes of 172nd Meeting of the Central Official Language Implementation Committee of RBI - *Rajbhasha Department*
11. Proceedings of the Meeting of the Local Board Western Area held on September 15, 2020 - *Secretary's Department*
12. Proceedings of the Meeting of the Local Board Northern Area) held on September 18, 2020 - *Secretary's Department*
13. Date and place of next Central Board Meeting - February 2021 at New Delhi - *Secretary's Department*

**Information Items:**

i. Change in the Financial Year, Change in the unit of Presentation and Revision in contents of the Balance Sheet, Income Statement and Weekly Statement of Affairs (WsA) of Reserve Bank of India (RBI) - *Department of Government and Bank Accounts (DGBA)*

ii. Report on Trend and Progress of Banking in India - *Department of Economic and Policy Research (DEPR)*

iii. Compliance to the Directions of the Central Board - *Secretary's Department*

iv. Customer Education Initiatives and Customer Feedback on the resolution of Customer Complaints; Segregate Minutes of Meeting, Agenda and Action Points - *Consumer Education and Protection Department (CEPD)*
